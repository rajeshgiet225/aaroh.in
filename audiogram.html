AUDIOGRAM

Audiograms are used to diagnose and monitor hearing loss. Audiograms are created by plotting the thresholds at which a patient can hear various frequencies. The audiogram is a graphical display of the measurement of hearing loss. Frequency and intensity are the two attributes used to measure the loss. Hearing in the each ear is screened and the softest frequency and the intensity that could is identified in the ears are noted.
